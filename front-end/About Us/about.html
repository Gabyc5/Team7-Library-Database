About Us

At Cougar Chronicles, we take pride in being more than just a library; we are a vibrant center of learning, innovation, and connection for our community.

Our Mission

Our mission at Cougar Chronicles is to empower individuals of all ages and backgrounds to discover, explore, and grow through access to information, resources, and diverse programming. We strive to foster a love of reading, lifelong learning, and critical thinking skills that enrich lives and strengthen communities.

Our History

Established in 1999, Cougar Chronicles has been serving the Houston community for 25 years. From our humble beginnings as a small collection of books to our current state-of-the-art facility, we have continuously evolved to meet the changing needs of our patrons.

Our Collections

With our books, digital resources, and multimedia materials, Cougar Chronicles offers a comprehensive collection that spans various genres, subjects, and interests. Whether you're seeking classic literature, the latest bestsellers, academic research materials, or children's books, you'll find a wealth of resources to satisfy your curiosity and expand your horizons.

Our Services

Cougar Chronicles is more than just a repository of books; we offer a wide range of services to support the diverse needs of our patrons. From research assistance and technology access to educational programming and community events, we strive to provide resources and opportunities that empower individuals to learn, grow, and thrive.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of librarians, staff, and volunteers are passionate about serving our community and promoting literacy and learning. With their expertise, enthusiasm, and commitment to excellence, they ensure that Cougar Chronicles remains a welcoming and inclusive space for all.

Visit Us

We invite you to visit Cougar Chronicles and explore everything we have to offer. Whether you're seeking information, inspiration, or simply a quiet place to read and reflect, you'll find a warm welcome and endless possibilities awaiting you at our library.

Thank you for choosing Cougar Chronicles as your destination for discovery, connection, and enrichment. We look forward to serving you and being a valued part of your journey.
